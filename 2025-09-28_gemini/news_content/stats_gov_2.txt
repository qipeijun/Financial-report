According to the National Bureau of Statistics of China, in mid-September 2025, the market prices of important means of production in the circulation field showed mixed changes compared to early September 2025. Out of 50 monitored products across 9 categories, 25 experienced price increases, 23 saw price decreases, and 2 remained unchanged.[1]
