Title: Wave Of US Air Tankers Deploy To Europe

Source: ZeroHedge
Published: Mon, 29 Sep 2025 11:45:00 +0000
Summary: <span class="field field--name-title field--type-string field--label-hidden">Wave Of US Air Tankers Deploy To Europe</span>

            <div class="clearfix text-formatted field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field__item"><p>OSINT-focused X accounts cite flight tracking data that shows a wave of U.S. air tankers deploying to Europe. The timing coincides with President Trump's <a href="https://x.com/zerohedge/status/1972348874401694132">scheduled</a> last-minute meeting with senior military leadership in northern Virginia on Tuesday. Historical precedent is very notable here: the last comparable mass tanker deployment (<a href="https://www.zerohedge.com/military/wave-usaf-tankers-depart-america-and-deploy-east-amid-escalating-israel-iran-conflict">read report</a>) preceded U.S. strikes on <a href="https://www.zerohedge.com/geopolitical/force-posturing-six-b-2-stealth-bombers-en-route-indo-pacific-ahead-trumps-intel">Iran's nuclear infrastructure</a> by five days. </p>

<p>Notable and most credible X accounts have reported an immediate deployment of U.S. air tankers to Europe. </p>

<p>"<strong>Roughly a dozen U.S. Air Force KC-135R/T "Stratotanker" Aerial-Refueling Tankers are currently up from a number of airbases across the United States, heading northeast and preparing to cross the Atlantic towards Europe, with several appearing to be enroute to RAF Mildenhall in England</strong>," X account OSINTdefender said. The X user appears to have sourced Flight</p>
