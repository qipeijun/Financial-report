The content of the URL is a report from the National Bureau of Statistics of China titled "Growth of Value Added of Industrial Enterprises Above Designated Size in August 2025 was 5.2%".[1]

Key figures from the report include:
*   In August 2025, the value added of industrial enterprises above designated size increased by 5.2% year-on-year.[1]
*   On a month-on-month basis, the value added of industrial enterprises above designated size increased by 0.37% in August.[1]
*   From January to August 2025, the value added of industrial enterprises above designated size increased by 6.2% year-on-year.[1]

By major categories in August:
*   Mining industry increased by 5.1%.[1]
*   Manufacturing industry increased by 5.7%.[1]
*   Electricity, heat, gas, and water production and supply industry increased by 2.4%.[1]

By economic type in August:
*   State-holding enterprises increased by 4.7%.[1]
*   Share-holding enterprises increased by 6.0%.[1]
*   Enterprises funded by foreign investors and Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan investors increased by 2.3%.[1]
*   Private enterprises increased by 4.6%.[1]

Sources:
[1] 2025年8月份规模以上工业增加值增长5.2% - 国家统计局 (https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/zxfb/202509/t20250915_1961180.html)
