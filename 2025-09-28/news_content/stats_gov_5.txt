From January to August 2025, China's national fixed asset investment (excluding rural households) reached 326,111 billion yuan, marking a year-on-year increase of 0.5%[1]. However, private fixed asset investment saw a decrease of 2.3% during the same period[1]. On a month-on-month basis, fixed asset investment (excluding rural households) in August declined by 0.20%[1].

Key investment figures by sector include:
*   **Primary industry:** Increased by 5.5%[1].
*   **Secondary industry:** Increased by 7.6%, with industrial investment up 7.7%, manufacturing up 5.1%, and electricity, heat, gas, and water production and supply up 18.8%[1].
*   **Tertiary industry:** Decreased by 3.4%, though infrastructure investment (excluding electricity, heat, gas, and water production and supply) increased by 2.0%[1].

Regionally, investment in the eastern region decreased by 3.5% and the northeastern region by 6.0%, while the central and western regions saw increases of 2.5% and 2.3% respectively[1]. By registration type, domestic enterprises' investment grew by 0.5%, Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan enterprises by 2.3%, and foreign enterprises' investment decreased by 15.4%[1].

Sources:
[1] 2025年1—8月份全国固定资产投资增长0.5% - 国家统计局 (https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/zxfb/202509/t20250915_1961178.html)
