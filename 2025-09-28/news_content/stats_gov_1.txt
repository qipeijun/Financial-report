From January to August 2025, the total profits of industrial enterprises above designated size in China reached 46929.7 billion yuan, showing a year-on-year increase of 0.9%.[1]

Key highlights include:
*   **State-controlled enterprises** saw profits of 15156.5 billion yuan, a decrease of 1.7%.[1]
*   **Share-holding enterprises** achieved profits of 34931.9 billion yuan, up by 1.1%.[1]
*   **Foreign-funded and Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan-invested enterprises** reported profits of 11723.6 billion yuan, an increase of 0.9%.[1]
*   **Private enterprises** recorded profits of 13076.1 billion yuan, growing by 3.3%.[1]

By sector:
*   **Mining** profits decreased by 30.6% to 5661.1 billion yuan.[1]
*   **Manufacturing** profits increased by 7.4% to 35233.5 billion yuan.[1]
*   **Electricity, heat, gas, and water production and supply** profits rose by 9.4% to 6035.1 billion yuan.[1]

In August alone, industrial enterprise profits increased by 20.4%, a significant turnaround from the 1.5% decline in the previous month.[1]

Sources:
[1] 2025年1—8月份全国规模以上工业企业利润增长0.9% - 国家统计局 (https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/zxfb/202509/t20250927_1961400.html)
